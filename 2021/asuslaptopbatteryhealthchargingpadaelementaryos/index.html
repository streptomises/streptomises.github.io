asus laptop battery health charging pada elementaryos

Written by octaviyawn with ♥ on 25 March 2021 in 1 min

buka terminal pada folder battery-manager-linux

chmod +x limit.sh

./limit.sh 60

sudo nano /etc/crontab

masukan

@reboot root echo 60 > /sys/class/power_supply/BAT0/charge_control_end_threshold

lalu simpan
